One of my favorite hobbies is to cook for large groups of people. I specialize in large-scale meal planning and traditional recipe preparation for 50+ people. Some of my favorite recipes include arroz rojo, birria, and carne asada.

Keyboards

One of my favorite technical hobbies is keyboard building. I own a variety of custom keyboards, and I collect switches, keycaps, and actual keyboard housings. My favorite keyboards are CNC'ed out of aluminum and brass and are usually quite heavy. I harvest old keyboards(like Apple's AEKII) for vintage switches.

Tea

I spent a semester abroad in Hong Kong and during this time, I explored a great deal of Asia and gained an appreciation for many parts of the culture. One of my favorite pasttimes is drinking tea socially. My favorite type of tea is pu'er tea, but I also really enjoy oolong and hojicha. My current favorite tea is a Yunnan sourced pu'er tea I bought in Taiwan.
